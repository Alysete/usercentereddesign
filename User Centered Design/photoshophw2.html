User Centered Design 1052

Original Photo

Part 1: Crop

First, I cropped the photo so less of the sky was showing. I purposefully left the photograph landscape to keep the rule of thirds in mind with the wooden path.

Part 2: Brightness/Contrast

Second, I increased the brightness of the overall photo to achieve less shadows in the grassy area, then I added some contrast to get back the shadows in different areas, such as the refection on the water.

Part 3: Variations

Third, to play with the color a bit, I used the 'variations' tool. I was able to make the skye bluer and the grass greener.

Part 4: Curve

Lastly, I used the 'curve' tool to once again bring back some shadows in the grass area, as well as in the trees in the background.
