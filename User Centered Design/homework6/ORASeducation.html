Public Nights

The Oil Region Astronomical Observatory is open to the public the third Friday of every month (except December - closed) for its regular meeting and observing. Throughout the summer months, the third Saturday the observatory is open to the public (unless otherwise noted). There is no admission charge, but donations are appreciated. During Public Nights the Observatory's 14 inch telescope and other smaller telescopes are available for viewing after dark. Before dark, observatory tours and an educational presentation are conducted. Activities begin between 7:00 PM and 8:00 PM, depending on the time of year.

Private Tours

Private group tours are made available upon request and are modified to meet the needs of individual groups. We have provided tours for church groups, various community groups, and Girl Scout and Boy Scout groups. The cost of a private group visit is $20.00 per group and an additional $1.00 per person for groups over 15 people.

Monthly Meetings

The Astronomical Society conducts monthly meetings at the Oil Region Astronomical Observatory. Meetings are normally held on the third Friday of each month and begin at 7:30 PM. Following is a 5 - 10 minute President's Report an educational program. Our educational programs are suitable for members of the general public with an interest in astronomy and all meetings are free and open to the general public.
