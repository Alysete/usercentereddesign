Welcome to Oil Region Astronomical Society

About Oil Region Astronomical Society (ORAS):


ORAS is a non for profit educational center with focus on astronomy. It was established in 1991 by a small group of people with a passion for astronomy.

For making the observatory possible, we'd like to thank: Samuel Justus Charitable Trust, Edith C. Justus Charitable Trust, John Nesbit Rees and Sarah Henne Rees Charitable Foundation, the Mellon Trust, and the Dr. and Mrs. Arthur William Phillips Charitable Trust.

As well as those listed, over 100 businesses and individuals throughout the community have donated through time, labor, materials,equipment, and funding. You have made a community's dream of an observatory, a reality!
